Holiday Island is famous for watersports like Water-Skiing, Jet Skiing, Scuba Diving, Wake-Boarding, Windsurfing and Sailing. There are sailing competitions every year in the island which is a epic moment to be around as you get to see competitors from all other the world.

Surfing

Surfing is the most popular water sport till date all around the world. Everyone loves to surfer and ride on the waves. Holiday Island is the perfect location for all the surfers. Its geographical location has been known for welcoming and attracting surfers all across the world for its quality of the waves making it one of the best surfing locations.
