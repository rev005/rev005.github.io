Geography

The Holiday Island resort is situated on the beautiful white-sands of Hawaii. It extends across the north western coastal belt of Kailua-Kona of Hawaii for a stretch of 150 acers. The resort is equipped with the amazing view of the vast blue waters. It attracts visitors all around the year and mostly during the summer. The resort is surrounded by a large number of lush green palm trees while perfectly reflecting the culture and the amazing nature of the topical Hawaii. It is a great treat to the visitors.

Culture

The traditional Hula dance of Hawaii is a perfect epitome of grace and beauty. The graceful movements of the dance capture the true spirit of the Hawaii culture. The music used for the dance is called Mele. The instruments used to produce this music are made from natural materials. The Merrie Monarch Hula festival is celebrated for a whole week which involves art exhibits of the traditional arts, parades and hula dance competition. Aloha festival is the most famous and oldest festival celebrated in Hawaii. It sure is a captivating experience for the visitors..

Services

The resort provides many services to its guests like Gym, Swimming pool, Shopping, Theater, Clubs, Spa and many more. The guests are provided with 24/7 room service. There are a total of 10 swimming pool for people of different ages. The resort is equipped with many fun and interactive activities for family and people of all ages. Camping with bonfire and barbeque are also provided for the guests.

Activities

Here at Holiday Island we provide the most amazing services for our visitors. Activities like Watersports, Spa and many other are provided to make your trip the most memorable with all the best experiences ever. We are famous for watersports like Water-Skiing, Jet Skiing, Scuba Diving, Wake-Boarding, Windsurfing and Sailing. There are sailing competitions every year in the island which is a epic moment to be around as you get to see competitors from all other the world.
